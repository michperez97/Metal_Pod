# Notes for App Store Review Team

## App Description
Metal Pod is a single-player offline hovercraft racing game. Players navigate obstacle courses using tilt controls and touch input.

## How to Test
1. Launch the app to open the Main Menu.
2. Tap `Play` to enter the Workshop.
3. Select `Inferno Gate` (first course, unlocked by default).
4. Tap `Launch` to start the race.
5. Tilt the device to steer; tap boost and brake controls during gameplay.
6. Complete the course to earn currency and medals.
7. Return to Workshop to upgrade or customize the hovercraft.

## Key Points
- No login required (offline game).
- No in-app purchases.
- No ads.
- No network dependency for normal gameplay.
- Accelerometer is used for tilt controls (physical device required).

## Demo Account
Not applicable. No accounts or authentication are used.

## Hardware Requirements
- Accelerometer required for tilt steering (best tested on physical iPhone/iPad).
- Haptic feedback uses Taptic Engine hardware where available.

## Content Rating Justification
- Recommended rating: 9+
- Contains infrequent/mild cartoon or fantasy violence
- Violence is limited to hovercraft damage from environmental hazards (lava, ice, toxic, explosions)
- No blood, gore, or realistic violence
- No violence against human characters

## Additional Reviewer Notes
- If testing in Simulator, accelerometer behavior is limited.
- All progression data is stored locally on-device.
- App is intended for landscape orientation only.
