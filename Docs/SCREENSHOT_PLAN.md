## App Icon Notes
- Source icon: 1024x1024 PNG, no alpha channel
- No text on icon (Apple guideline)
- No rounded corners in source (iOS applies corner mask)
- Keep central subject clear at small sizes

## Capture Quality Notes
- Disable debug overlays and editor gizmos.
- Capture at native render size, then crop to App Store aspect as needed.
- Ensure text overlays do not cover critical gameplay HUD cues.
