TOXIC ZONE - Survive acid pools, electric fences, and exploding barrels.

Visit the Workshop to tune your hovercraft with 4 upgrade categories:

- Armor - Take more hits before destruction
- Handling - Tighter turns and better control
- Boost - Longer, more powerful bursts
